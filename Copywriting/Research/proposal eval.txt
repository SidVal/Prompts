TASK 1 Use this heading “General Evaluation” and answer the following questions but do not repeat the question.
Heading “Clarity”
Question:  What is the overall clarity and coherence of the proposal. Does the proposal clearly state the research objectives and methodology?
Heading “Objective Clearly Defined?”
Question:  Are the research aims and objectives well-defined and aligned with the project's significance?
Heading “Lit Review?”
Question:  How comprehensive is the literature review section? Are key references and related work adequately covered?

Heading “Other Comments”
Question:  Are the research methods appropriate and well-described? Do they address potential limitations?
Question:  Are the proposed timelines and milestones realistic and achievable?
Question:  Is the budget justified and reasonable for the proposed research activities?
Question:  Are there any potential ethical considerations or risks associated with the proposed research?
Question:  Are the expected outcomes and deliverables clearly outlined?
Question:  Are there any additional suggestions or recommendations to enhance the proposal's overall quality?
Please provide a thorough evaluation, including specific feedback and suggestions for improvement, based on your expertise and analysis.
